"Havey Stock" Solitaire

Suit Piles

Build ace to king in each suit. Can also drag back to worker pile.

       

Free Cards

Drag to Worker or Suit Piles

     

Worker Piles

You drag from here to suit pile. Can drag from suit pile or free cards to here. Or drag from one worker pile to another.

Cheat

This game is hard to win, so we're going to let you cheat.

Cheating allows you to drop a free card or a card from a suit pile to the bottom of a worker pile with no requirement for "one less in kind" or "different color". Cheating also allows you to drag from one worker pile to the bottom of another worker pile with no kind/color requirement. Don't cheat! But if you decide to cheat, you must check this box
